# PPC Account Validation Framework  

I approach PPC campaign management with a structured, analytical mindset focused on account organization, testing methodology, and measurable optimization.

This framework demonstrates how I evaluate, validate, and optimize Google Ads Search campaigns through disciplined account architecture, controlled experimentation, performance analysis, and scalable optimization practices.

Drawing from a background in Quality Assurance (ISTQB® Certified), I apply systems thinking and analytical rigor to PPC campaign management, emphasizing data accuracy, controlled testing, and sustainable performance improvement.

---

## 1. Campaign Type
- Prioritize Search campaigns for high intent demand.
- Avoid premature expansion into Display, Video, or Performance Max.

## 2. Network Settings
- Launch with Google Search only.
- Exclude Search Partners to protect data quality.
- Expand networks only after performance validation.

## 3. Geographic Targeting
- Target core service areas only.
- Avoid overly broad targeting.
- Monitor performance by location and apply bid adjustments where justified.

## 4. Ad Scheduling
- Align ads with business hours and lead capacity.
- Evaluate time-of-day performance.
- Reduce spend during low conversion windows.

## 5. Match Type Strategy
- Use controlled match types at launch.
- Avoid unrestricted broad targeting.
- Expand based on validated search term data.

## 6. Search Term Analysis
- Analyze actual queries weekly.
- Identify intent drift.
- Promote high performing terms to exact match.

## 7. Negative Keyword Management
- Exclude low intent modifiers (free, jobs, how to).
- Use cross ad group negatives to prevent overlap.
- Treat negatives as an ongoing optimization lever.

## 8. Automation Control
- Collect statistically meaningful data before smart bidding.
- Validate CPA trends before scaling.
- Introduce automation responsibly.

## 9. Account Structure
- Segment campaigns by primary service.
- Maintain tightly themed ad groups.
- Protect Quality Score through relevance alignment.

## 10. Landing Page & Ad Rank Alignment
- Ensure keyword, ad, and landing page congruence.
- Maintain message continuity.
- Optimize for conversion probability.

## 11. Search Impression Share & Positioning
- Monitor:
  - Search Impression Share
  - IS Lost (Budget)
  - IS Lost (Rank)
  - Absolute Top Impression Share
- Use visibility data to guide bid and budget decisions.

## 12. Device Segmentation
- Evaluate mobile and desktop independently.
- Monitor conversion rate, CPC, and engagement differences.
- Apply device bid adjustments when statistically justified.

## 13. Remarketing Layer
- Deploy after validating Search performance.
- Re-engage high intent visitors.
- Improve overall conversion efficiency.

## 14. Ad Assets
- Implement sitelinks, callouts, and structured snippets.
- Maximize ad real estate and CTR.

## Framework Philosophy

- Deploy under controlled conditions  
- Validate performance signals  
- Identify root causes  
- Iterate based on measurable data  
- Scale responsibly  

The goal is structured, testable, scalable performance growth.
